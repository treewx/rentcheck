How to Find Your Akahu Tokens

Step 1: Go to Akahu Personal Apps

Visit https://my.akahu.nz/login and log in to your Akahu account.

Step 2: Create or Select Your Personal App

If you don't have a personal app yet, create one by clicking "Create Personal App". Give it a name like "RentCheck".

Step 3: Find Your Tokens

Navigate to the Developers section to find your tokens:

Click on "Developers" in the sidebar menu

Once you're in the Developers section, you'll see your tokens clearly displayed:

Look for the App Token and User Token fields

  • App Token: Starts with "app_token_" - This identifies your RentCheck app
  • User Token: Starts with "user_token_" - This gives access to your bank accounts

Step 4: Copy the Tokens

Click the copy button next to each token and paste them into the RentCheck settings form below.
